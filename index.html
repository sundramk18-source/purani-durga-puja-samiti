Shardiya Navratri 2025

The Members of Purani Durga Puja Samiti, Dhurlakh cordially invite you to join the celebrations.

22nd Sep - 1st Oct 2025

Puja Schedule

Join us in our daily rituals and celebrations.

प्रति​पदा (22 Sep, Mon)

Mata Shailputri Puja after Ghat Sthapana.

द्वितीया (23 Sep, Tue)

Maa Brahmacharini Puja with Chandra Darshan.

तृतीया (24 Sep, Wed)

Worship of Maa Chandraghanta.

चतुर्थी (25 Sep, Thu)

Worship of Maa Kushmanda & Vinayak Chaturthi Vrat.

पंचमी (26 Sep, Fri)

Worship of Maa Skandamata.

षष्ठी (27 Sep, Sat)

Worship of Mata Katyayani.

सप्तमी (28 Sep, Sun)

Worship of Maa Kalratri.

अष्टमी (29 Sep, Mon)

Worship of Maa Mahagauri.

नवमी (30 Sep, Tue)

Navami Havan, Ayudh Puja, and Kanya Pujan.

दशमी (01 Oct, Wed)

Vijayadashami.
